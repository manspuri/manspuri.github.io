How to Make a Great Cup of Tea

There's something to be said for morning rituals. Some people's mornings begin with a workout, others with some stretching, a cup of coffee, or a bowl of cereal. My day starts with a cup of tea.

The steps for making a cup of tea are fairly simple:

  1. Boil water in a kettle
  2. Add teabag or loose-leaf tea to cup
  3. Add boiling water to cup
  4. Add milk and/or sweetener of your choice

But there are conditions which some tea-lovers have come to treat as absolutely necessary for a proper cup of tea. I've adopted a few of these requirements and incorporated them into my tea-making ritual (notice the order):

  1. Boil two cups of water in a kettle
  2. Add sugar to your teacup for desired sweetness; teacup should be ceramic (not paper, plastic, or metal)
  3. Add boiling water to teacup
  4. Add PG Tips black teabag to teacup of boiled water
  5. Add heated milk after tea has brewed for 1 minute

Feel free to incorporate some of Mr. Orwell's requirements (or your own additions) to your tea-making process!
