Website Plan

My current website needs the following improvements:

  • Better color scheme

    • Right now, the color scheme is bland; I plan on adding a high-resolution image as the entire background of the webpage. The font will need to stand out with the image. I will do this by experimenting with colors as well as font sizes.

  • Visible Links

    • The current links are very close to each other and the font is small. This makes it difficult for users to differentiate between links or to even click on a link. I want to make the links larger and stacked on top of each other rather an in a row. I will also change the weight of the link's font when one hovers over the link.

  • Obvious Separation Between Sections

    • Currently, all sections are very close to one another. I would like to separate the sections using floats, so that users are aware of the differences between each section (i.e. a divided space for blogposts and another for social media links).

I am toying with the idea of having a link for a page which lists all blog-post entries. This will eventually be necessary as the list of blog-post entries increase. I also think that I should include a separate link for an 'About Me' section.
