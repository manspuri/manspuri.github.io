Exploring My Values

Honesty, Creativity, Affection, Music, Self-knowledge, Self-reliance, Self-respect, Will-power, Grit, Openness, Movement

I don't know if I can say that one particular value is more important to me than another of the ones which I have listed. However, 'honesty' is a value which I strive towards every day. I think that honesty is incredibly powerful and liberating. When I speak honestly, I feel both open, because I am not hiding anything but protected, because I have nothing to hide. More directly, I think that being honest when providing advice ultimately benefits the person seeking advice.

I hope that I have can be honest with myself about my understanding of the material while at DBC. I also hope that I have the strength to ask others for their honest feedback and opinions about my progress. I hope that I can continue to recognize honesty as a value during the next 11 weeks, even if it is difficult to hear.
